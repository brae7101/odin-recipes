Guacamole

Description

A wonderful homemade guacamole recipe, utilizing fresh ingredients that will have your guests craving more!

ingredients

  • 3 Ripe Avocados
  • 1 or 2 Jalapenos
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 roma tomatoes
  • half of a diced red onion
  • 1 lime worth of lime juice
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Half the avacados and remove the pit. Scoop avacado from peels
  2. Mash avacado in bowl making sure to not over mash
  3. Dice tomatoes and half red onion and add to bowl
  4. Remove core of jalapeno and seeds, dice and add to bowl
  5. Mince garlic cloves and add to bowl
  6. Squeeze the juice of one lime, stir until all ingredients well distributed
  7. Serve with tortilla chips and enjoy!

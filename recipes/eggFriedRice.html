Egg Fried Rice

Description

A simple yet delcious egg fried rice recipe perfect for pairing with a protein of your choosing.

ingredients

  • 2 cups day old rice
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 green onion
  • Half tsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  1. Heat vegetable oil in wok over medium high heat
  2. Add eggs and scramble
  3. Once eggs take shape, add rice and mix
  4. Move egg and rice mixture to one side of the wok, add garlic and onion into voided area.
  5. after 30 seconds combine garlic and onions to the egg and rice making sure all ingredients are combined well
  6. Add in sesame oil, soy sauce and sugar. Stir wok well.
  7. Cook until desired doneness. Rice should have a decent crust from sauce and sugar.
